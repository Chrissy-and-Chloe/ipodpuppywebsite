1. COMMERCIAL USE
If your commission will be used for commercial purposes (e.g., designs for merchandise, album covers, posters for sale), please indicate this upfront. Commercial usage may incur additional fees to reflect its intended use, or we can discuss a potential royalty arrangement.

2. COMMISSION CONTENT
If you're unsure whether your request fits my skillset (e.g., complex detail work, mecha, long-form project), please ask me beforehand. I'll let you know if it's something I can accommodate or falls outside my scope.

3. PAYMENT TERMS
Payments can be processed through Venmo, Cashapp, and Paypal. If these aren't options available to you, let me know so we can explore an alternative that works.

If you need your commission completed quickly or want priority placement in my queue, a rush fee will apply. This is calculated as 20% of the original price. For example, a $100 project would include an additional $20, making the total $120.

6. COPYRIGHT AND USAGE RIGHTS
As an artist, I retain the copyright to all commissioned work unless otherwise agreed upon. This includes the right to display the artwork in my portfolio, social media, or other platforms unless the client requests confidentiality.
Clients may not claim my work as their own or alter it without permission. For personal use, you are free to share and display the artwork with proper credit to me as the creator.

7. REFUNDS AND CANCELLATIONS
Refunds are available only before significant progress has been made on your commission. If I‚Äôve started the work, a partial refund may be issued based on how much work has been completed.

8. DEADLINES
If your commission has a specific deadline, please communicate this clearly before the project begins. I will confirm if I can accommodate your timeline, and if necessary, a rush fee may apply.

9. REVISIONS
Minor revisions are included in the price. However, significant changes or multiple revisions beyond the original request may result in additional fees.

10. DELIVERY
Completed commissions will be delivered digitally via email. If specific file types or dimensions are required, please specify them in advance.

11. FILE STORAGE
I will keep your commission files for up to 3 months after delivery. After this period, I cannot guarantee the files will still be available. Make sure to save copies of all files once they are delivered!

12. AI USAGE AND NFT POLICY
My artwork may not be used in any AI training programs or as input for AI-generated content. Additionally, my work may not be minted or sold as NFTs (non-fungible tokens) under any circumstances.

14. UPFRONT PAYMENT POLICY
Commissions require a 50% upfront deposit before work begins. The remaining balance is due upon completion before final files are delivered.

15. QUEUE AND WORKLOAD
Commissions are completed in the order they are received unless a rush fee is applied. I will inform you of your approximate position in the queue and estimated completion time when we begin.

16. DISPUTES
If there are any issues with the completed commission, please notify me promptly. I will do my best to resolve the matter, but disputes handled outside this agreement (e.g., through chargebacks) may result in a ban from future commissions.
